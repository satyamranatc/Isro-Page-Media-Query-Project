Exploring Space, Empowering India

ISRO leads India's space exploration program with innovative missions, satellite technology, and space research to benefit society and advance scientific knowledge.

Chandrayaan-3

India's successful lunar mission that made a historic soft landing on the Moon's south pole, making India the fourth country to achieve a lunar landing.

Gaganyaan

India's first human spaceflight program, aimed at demonstrating human spaceflight capability to low earth orbit.

Aditya-L1

India's first space-based solar observatory to study the Sun's corona, solar emissions, and solar winds.

Latest Achievements

ISRO continues to break new grounds in space exploration with successful missions like Chandrayaan-3's lunar landing, the Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan), and the development of indigenous launch vehicles.

Our focus remains on harnessing space technology for national development while pursuing planetary exploration to push the boundaries of human knowledge.
